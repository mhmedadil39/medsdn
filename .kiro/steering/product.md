# Product Overview

MedSDN is an open-source Laravel-based eCommerce platform designed specifically for medical and healthcare products. Built on a scalable architecture, it provides a complete eCommerce solution with support for multi-vendor marketplaces, multi-tenant SaaS platforms, headless commerce, and mobile commerce.

## Core Capabilities

- Full-featured eCommerce platform with product catalog, cart, checkout, and order management
- Medical and healthcare product specialization
- Multi-vendor marketplace support with seller management and commission handling
- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture for building marketplace platforms
- Headless commerce with Next.js integration
- Mobile commerce with Flutter-based mobile apps
- AI-powered features (chatbots, product descriptions, recommendations)
- Blockchain/decentralized commerce support (NFT marketplaces, smart contracts)
- Point of Sale (POS) system for retail operations
- 100+ pre-built extensions available

## Target Use Cases

- Building medical and healthcare online stores
- Migrating physical pharmacies and medical stores to online platforms
- Creating multi-vendor medical marketplaces
- Developing SaaS-based healthcare eCommerce platforms
- Implementing headless commerce solutions for medical products
- Building mobile-first healthcare commerce applications
